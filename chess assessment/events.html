Upcoming Events

At the Grandmasters Chess Club, we host a variety of events throughout the year to bring our members together, improve our skills, and share the joy of chess. From friendly matches to competitive tournaments, there’s something for every player.

  • Weekly Practice Sessions - Every Thursday evening, members gather for casual games, analysis of famous matches, and one-on-one coaching opportunities.
  • Monthly Club Tournaments - A chance to test your skills against other members in a structured and friendly competition. Prizes are awarded for top performers!
  • Strategy Workshops - Learn advanced tactics, endgame techniques, and opening theory from experienced players and guest instructors.
  • Special Guest Events - Occasionally, we invite chess masters and experienced tournament players to give talks, demonstrations, and simultaneous exhibition matches.

Whether you’re looking to compete, learn, or simply enjoy the game with friends, our events are designed to be fun, welcoming, and enriching for everyone.
